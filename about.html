About MrsMillennial Designs

Crafted with love by Andrea

Hi, I'm Andrea

Welcome to MrsMillennial Designs! I'm a designer, teacher, and mom who creates meaningful printables that blend modern style with timeless sentimentality.

I specialize in handprint keepsakes that help parents capture precious moments, classroom resources that brighten learning spaces, and digital art prints that add a personal touch to any home.

What started as crafts for my own 1st grade classroom and my toddler has grown into a shop with 149 designs and 778 five-star reviews from happy customers around the world.

Every design is created with love and care. Thank you for supporting our small business!
