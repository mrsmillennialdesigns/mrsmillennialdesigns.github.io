Meet Andrea

The heart, hands, and wildflower soul behind MrsMillennial Designs

"I believe every child's handprint tells a story, and every classroom deserves to feel like a place where magic happens."

A Designer and a Mom

Andrea is a designer, a mom, and a lover of wildflowers. What started as a simple handprint craft with her own children grew into a passion for creating beautiful digital art that families and teachers use to make lasting memories.

Every design in the MrsMillennial Designs shop is thoughtfully crafted with a watercolor, wildflower aesthetic. Andrea pours her heart into each template, each print, each tiny detail -- because she knows these designs end up framed on walls, tucked into memory boxes, and treasured for years.

The Wildflower Aesthetic

Andrea's signature style blends soft watercolors, delicate florals, and warm, inviting tones. She draws inspiration from nature -- wildflower meadows, sun-dappled gardens, and the simple beauty of a child's painted hand pressed onto paper.

Her designs bring that same sense of warmth and wonder to everything from Mother's Day handprint crafts to classroom leadership posters. Whether you're a parent looking for a meaningful keepsake or a teacher looking to inspire, Andrea's designs help you create something beautiful.

Why Digital?

Digital downloads mean you can start creating the moment inspiration strikes. No shipping wait times, no extra costs -- just download, print, and let the creativity flow. Need to print again after a paint spill? No problem. Want to share with a friend? Easy. Andrea designed her shop this way because she believes beautiful art should be accessible to everyone.

What She Creates

Andrea creates beautiful digital art, handprint templates, and classroom resources. Her designs help parents create meaningful keepsakes and help teachers brighten their classrooms. From personalized birth flower prints to 7 Habits classroom posters, every design is made with care and purpose.
