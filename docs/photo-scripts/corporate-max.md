# Corporate Gathering — MAX Shot Script (~58 shots)

**For the Lead Coordinator** — Print this and keep it in your pocket.

---

## Quick Tips for All Photographers (Phone Users)
- Clean your lens before shooting.
- Landscape for groups and venue. Portrait for speakers and individuals.
- Tap to focus on faces. No flash for candids. Take 3-5 of every shot.
- Get close — zoom with your feet, not pinch-zoom.
- For speakers: 45-degree angle to get face AND crowd behind them.
- Back up photos during breaks — upload to shared album.
