Privacy Policy

MrsMillennial Designs — Last updated: March 7, 2026

Overview

MrsMillennial Designs ("we", "us") operates the website mrsmillennialdesigns.com. We sell digital art, printable keepsakes, and classroom resources as instant digital downloads. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

When you make a purchase, our payment processor (Stripe) collects:

  • Contact info: Name and email address (for order confirmation and digital delivery)
  • Payment info: Credit/debit card details (processed securely by Stripe — we never see or store your full card number)

When browsing our website, we do not collect:

  • No cookies or tracking pixels
  • No analytics or behavioral tracking
  • No personal information unless you make a purchase

How We Use Your Information

  • To process your payment and deliver your digital download
  • To send your order confirmation email
  • To respond if you contact us about an order

We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or any other purpose.

Payment Processing

All payments are processed by Stripe, a PCI-DSS Level 1 certified payment processor. Your payment details are encrypted and transmitted directly to Stripe's servers. We never have access to your full card number. See Stripe's Privacy Policy.

Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers or data brokers
  • Use tracking pixels, cookies, or analytics services
  • Store your payment information on our servers

The only third party that receives your data is Stripe, solely for payment processing.

Data Retention

Order records (name, email, purchase details) are retained by Stripe according to their data retention policies. We may keep order records for tax and accounting purposes as required by law.

Digital Products & Refunds

All products are instant digital downloads. Due to the nature of digital goods, we generally do not offer refunds once a file has been downloaded. If you experience issues with your download, please contact us and we will work to resolve the problem.

Security

  • All website traffic is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
  • Payment processing uses Stripe's PCI-DSS Level 1 certified infrastructure
  • Content Security Policy headers protect against XSS attacks
  • No sensitive data is stored on our website servers

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Your Rights

You may request to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Correct inaccurate information

Contact us at the address below to exercise these rights.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our business evolves. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or your order, email us at: mrs.millennialdesigns@outlook.com
