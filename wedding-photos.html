Common Questions

How many photos do we need for a good album?
We recommend 10+ guests each taking 20+ photos, giving you 200–1,000+ shots to work with. More is always better — we curate the best ones for your album. With the QR codes making it so easy, most couples end up with way more than they expected.
What if some photos are blurry or low quality?
Our editing process catches and fixes most quality issues. Truly unusable photos are filtered out during curation. Modern smartphone cameras are incredibly capable — with 200+ shots from your guests, you'll have more than enough great ones to fill your album.
Can I choose which photos go in the album?
Yes! After we edit the photos, we send you a preview of the album layout. You can request changes, swap photos, or rearrange pages before we send it to print. It's your album — you get the final say.
How long does shipping take?
Your hardcover album ships within 2 weeks of your wedding. The hanging poster print (Max package) ships separately within 1–2 weeks. Both ship tracked to your door with free shipping included.
Can I use this alongside a traditional photographer?
Absolutely! Many couples use our package as a complement to their photographer. Guest photos capture candid angles, behind-the-scenes moments, and perspectives a single photographer simply can't cover. It's like having 50 extra cameras at your wedding.
What exactly does the lead coordinator do?
They follow our detailed shot script to make sure every key moment is captured — the vows, the first kiss, the cake cutting, family portraits. Think of them as the director, while all your guests are the camera crew. No experience needed — the script tells them exactly what to do and when.
