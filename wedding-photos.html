How It Works

Order Your Package

Choose Simple or Max and complete your order. We'll email you your shot script, QR code printouts, and a lead coordinator guide within 24 hours.

2

Assign Your Lead Coordinator

Pick one trusted friend or family member to follow the shot script. They don't need to be a photographer — we give them everything they need.

3

Print the QR codes and place them on tables, at the bar, near the dance floor. Encourage every guest to scan and upload their photos throughout the night.

After your wedding, we collect all uploaded photos, professionally edit every shot, design your album layout, and ship your hardcover book within 2 weeks.

Common Questions

How many photos do we need?
We recommend 10+ guests each taking 20+ photos, giving you 200–1,000+ shots to work with. More is always better — we'll curate the best ones for your album.
What if some photos are blurry or low quality?
Our editing process catches and fixes most quality issues. Truly unusable photos are filtered out during the curation process. With 200+ shots, you'll have plenty of great ones.
Can I choose which photos go in the album?
Yes! After we edit the photos, we'll send you a preview of the album layout. You can request changes before we send it to print.
How long does shipping take?
Your hardcover album ships within 2 weeks of your wedding. Canvas prints (Max package) ship separately within 1–2 weeks. Both ship tracked to your door.
Can I use this alongside a traditional photographer?
Absolutely! Many couples use our package as a complement to their photographer. Guest photos capture candid angles and moments a single photographer can't be in two places to get.
What does the lead coordinator do?
They follow our detailed shot script to make sure every key moment is captured — the vows, the first kiss, the cake cutting, family portraits. Think of them as the director, while all the guests are the camera crew.
