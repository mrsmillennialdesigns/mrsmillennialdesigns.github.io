Printing Tips

For the best results with your digital download:

  • Print on cardstock for keepsakes and crafts
  • Use "Actual Size" in your print settings (not "Fit to Page")
  • For best color accuracy, use a color laser printer or professional print shop
  • Most designs are 8.5x11" or 8x10" — check your product description for sizes
