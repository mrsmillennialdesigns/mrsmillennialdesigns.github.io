Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download my purchase?
After payment, you'll receive an email with a download link for your files. Check your spam folder if you don't see it within a few minutes. Files are delivered as high-resolution PDF, JPG, PNG, or SVG.
Can you customize an existing design?
Yes! Andrea can change names, dates, colors, text, and more on any design. Custom edits start at $5. See all custom order options.
How fast can I get a rush order?
Rush orders are guaranteed within 24 hours of payment. Add $10 to any order for rush service. Learn more.
What sizes are the printables?
Most designs come in 8x10 and 8.5x11 sizes. Some include additional sizes like 11x17 or 16x20. The available sizes are listed on each product page.
Can I print at home or do I need a print shop?
You can print at home on regular paper or cardstock. For best results, use cardstock paper and a color inkjet printer. You can also take the files to any print shop like Staples, FedEx, or Walgreens.
Do you offer bulk / classroom discounts?
Yes! Orders of 10+ copies get 20% off. Email us with what you need and we'll send a custom quote. See bulk pricing.
