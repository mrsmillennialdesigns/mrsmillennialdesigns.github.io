What Happens Now

1

Check your email — You'll receive a confirmation receipt from Stripe within a few minutes. We'll also send your shot script and QR code files within 24 hours.

2

Share the script with your coordinator — Pick one trusted person to follow the shot script during your event. Send them the PDF we provide — it tells them exactly what to capture and when.

3

Print or display the QR codes — Place them on tables, at the entrance, near the food. The more guests who scan and upload, the better your album will be.

4

Enjoy your event! — After your event, we'll collect all uploaded photos, professionally edit them, design your album, and ship everything to your door within 2–3 weeks.

Your Downloads

Your shot script and QR code files will be emailed to you within 24 hours of purchase. If you ordered a QR Poster, it will be shipped separately and arrive before your event (allow 5–7 business days).
