How It Works

1

Order Your Package

Choose Simple or Max and tell us your event type. We'll email you a tailored shot script, QR code printouts, and a lead coordinator guide within 24 hours.

2

Assign Your Lead Coordinator

Pick one trusted friend, family member, or colleague to follow the shot script. They don't need to be a photographer — we give them everything they need to capture the key moments.

3

Set Up QR Codes at Your Venue

Print and place QR codes on tables, at the entrance, near the food — wherever guests gather. Everyone scans and uploads their photos instantly. No app needed.

4

We Edit, Design & Ship

After your event, we collect all uploaded photos, professionally edit every shot, design your album layout, and ship your photo book — all within 2 weeks. Free shipping included.

Common Questions

What types of events do you cover?
Birthday parties (kids and adults), anniversary celebrations, corporate events and galas, church and community gatherings, newborn photo sessions, engagement parties, retirement parties, reunions, graduation celebrations — if people are gathering to celebrate, we cover it.
How does the newborn session package work?
Same concept, smaller scale. You designate 1–2 family members as photographers, we provide a newborn-specific shot script with poses, angles, and lighting tips for at-home sessions. They follow the script, upload the photos through the QR code, and we handle everything from there — editing, album layout, and shipping.
How many photos do we need?
For larger events (birthdays, corporate, church), we recommend 10+ guests each taking 20+ photos for 200–1,000+ shots. For intimate events like newborn sessions, 50–150 photos is plenty. More is always better — we curate the best ones for your album.
What's the difference between Simple and Max?
Simple gives you a softcover book (8x11", 30 pages) with digital downloads and a ~27-shot script. Max upgrades to a hardcover book (8x8", 60 pages) with a custom cover, a 20x28" hanging poster print on a wood hanger, a detailed ~55-shot script, and a lead coordinator cheat sheet. Both include free shipping.
What is the QR Poster add-on?
For $14.99, we print and mail a beautiful, event-ready QR poster directly to you before your event. Order at least 2 weeks in advance for guaranteed delivery. If you need it sooner, choose the digital download option and print it yourself at any print shop or on your home printer.
How long until I receive my album?
Your photo book ships within 2 weeks of your event. The hanging poster print (Max package) ships separately within 1–2 weeks. Both ship tracked to your door with free shipping. We'll send you a preview of the album layout before printing so you can request changes.
